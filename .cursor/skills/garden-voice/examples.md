## `key_concept` - claim first, no links on line 1

### ❌ No punch - reads like a summary

> Note systems should use plain files and open formats with regular backups so that application shutdown does not cause data loss.

### ✅ Claim you'd argue (`anti-fragile-systems.md` shape)

> Plain files outlive every app I've trusted.
>
> I build on markdown and exports I actually open - not a vendor promise that dies on acquisition day.
> [[Future-Proofing Knowledge]] names the habit; [[Antifragility]] names why stress should make the system stronger, not brittle.

### ❌ Wikilink on line 1

> [[Free Grace]] settles where I spend forever.

### ✅ Plain claim first, links after (`sanctification.md` shape)

> Sanctification settles who I'm becoming on the way.
>
> [[Grateful Obedience]] names the posture: grow from thanks, not to earn acceptance.
> [[Standing vs Fellowship]] keeps a fruitless week from feeling like revoked standing.

> Strategic foresight is the practice of looking past the immediate move to what the board will look like in six months.

>
> [[Reversibility]] is the decision frame; rollback is the rehearsed move.

## `shareable_thought` - four angles, not fragments

### ❌ Fragments

> "Growth, not the ticket."
> "Slow walk."
> "Christlikeness."
> "Discipleship daily."

### ❌ Four restatements of `description`

> Eternal life is Christ's gift by faith alone.
> Eternal life comes by faith, not works.
> Faith alone receives eternal life.
> Christ's gift is eternal life through faith.

### ✅ Four angles (`blameless-after-action-review.md`)

> After rollback restores service, I learn what broke in the system - not who to punish for pushing deploy.
> Rollback Principle gets users back on the old build; blameless after-action review gets judgment back.
> Own the Error still means someone says "my call" in standup; the room fixes the runbook, not the person.
> Fix the runbook after rollback - not the person who pushed deploy.

Angles: **when** (after calm), **pairing** (rollback + review), **culture** (own the error), **move** (fix runbook).

## Vocabulary - skill stricter than lint

Lint may allow `landscape` or `robust` in existing prose. **New drafts** still cut them unless the term is unavoidable (product name, theology term after gloss).

### ❌ Thesaurus

> Navigate the landscape of capture to facilitate optimal knowledge dissemination.

### ✅ Plain

> One inbox. Save what hits you. Empty it weekly.
