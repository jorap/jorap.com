Markdown formatting is **structure without a WYSIWYG**. I use the same habits in Hugo posts and wiki tiddlers: break early, heading every scroll, code fences for commands.

Bad formatting wastes the best ideas behind a wall of text.

---

## One idea per paragraph

Three to five lines max on web. **Enter key is your friend.**

---

## Heading hierarchy

Don't skip H2 to H4. Screen readers and TOC generators care. **Logical outline = readable outline.**

---

## Tables sparingly

Comparison tables help; wide tables break mobile. **Lists often beat tables** for notes.

---

## Bottom line

Markdown formatting is UX. **Format while you write**, not in a panic before publish. Future readers include you.
