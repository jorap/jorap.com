## Direction, pace, and care

- [[Leadership]] - own direction, pace, and care when people look your way - title optional

## Earning followership

- [[Influence Without Title]] - calm voice in the crisis; people follow trust before titles
- [[Title Without Influence]] - hollow badge; meetings that change nothing
- [[Servant Leadership]] - unblock, coach, take blame up, pass credit down
- [[Humility and Service]] - gospel depth; greatness through serving, not grasping

## Spending influence well

- [[Ethical Leadership]] - what you tolerate in the open becomes their real standard
- [[Follow Christ, Then Lead]] - when influence pressure and Christ's command collide, follow Christ first
- [[Discipleship vs Leadership]] - upward allegiance and outward care stay separate seats
