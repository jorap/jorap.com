## Prayer and solitude

Jesus prayed often, usually alone, often before the crowd's next demand.

- [[Withdraw to Pray]] - rose before dawn; often slipped to lonely places (Mark 1:35; Luke 5:16)
- [[Pray Before You Choose]] - all night in prayer before naming the twelve (Luke 6:12)
- [[Pray When Drained]] - alone on the mountain after feeding five thousand (Matthew 14:23; Mark 6:46)
- [[Jesus' Prayers]] - seven verbatim prayers the Gospels quote (thanksgiving, John 17:1-26, Gethsemane, the cross)

Garden links: [[Prayer Jesus Taught]] - teaching hub for Lord's Prayer, [[Secret Devotion]], persistence; [[Jesus' Prayers]] - verbatim words to the Father.

## Scripture

Jesus did not treat the Bible as decoration. He knew it, read it aloud, quoted it under pressure, and opened it for others.

- [[It Is Written]] - wilderness temptation answered from Deuteronomy (Matthew 4:1-11)
- [[Read in Synagogue]] - Sabbath custom; stood and read Isaiah aloud (Luke 4:16-21)
- [[Opened Scripture]] - explained Moses and the Prophets on the Emmaus road (Luke 24:27, 45)
- Read the Old Testament as the Father's story through Him (Luke 24:44)

Garden links: [[Renewed Mind]]; [[Abide in Me]]; [[Discern His Will Daily]].

## Worship, Sabbath, and feasts

- [[Sabbath for Good]] - Lord of the Sabbath; mercy over rule-keeping for show (Mark 2:27; Luke 13:16)
- Synagogue and temple; feasts in Jerusalem (Luke 2:49; John 2:13-17)

Garden links: [[Love God]] - whole-person devotion includes gathered worship, not only private reading.

## People and compassion

His calendar bent for real people. Depth with a few, open table with many.

- [[Moved with Compassion]] - saw the crowds; touched the leper (Matthew 9:36; Mark 1:41)
- [[Table Fellowship]] - ate with disciples and sinners (Mark 2:15; Luke 5:29-32)
- [[Invest in the Few]] - chose twelve; washed their feet (Mark 3:14; John 13:1-17)
- [[Kingdom on the Road]] - town to town teaching, preaching, healing (Luke 4:43; Matthew 9:35)

Garden links: [[Love Your Neighbor]]; [[Humility and Service]]; [[Peacemakers]]; [[Great Commission]]; [[Discipleship]].

## The Father's will

The engine under every rhythm: obedience to the Father, one day at a time.

- [[The Father's Food]] - "My food is to do the will of Him who sent Me" (John 4:34)
- Only what pleases the Father (John 5:19; 8:28-29)
- [[Take Up Your Cross]] - daily deny self and follow (Luke 9:23)

Garden links: [[Grateful Obedience]]; [[Present a Living Sacrifice]]; [[Seek the Kingdom First]].
