**Layered reading** = pass through the same source at increasing depth — skim, highlight, summarize.

## Examples

- **Sports:** First pass on the coaching manual marked three sections; second pass only those got sentences in my own words.
- **Non-technical work:** First pass marked three sections of the safety manual; second pass only those got sentences in my own words.
- **Home with children:** Skim the permission form, highlight the three dates that matter, summarize those on the fridge - layered reading is increasing depth, not one heroic read.
- **Real world:** First commute listen for the gist; second pass with a notebook on the parts I'd cite later - pass through the same source at increasing depth.
