## Thanksgiving and trust

- [[Thank Father for Revelation]] - thanks the Father for hiding truth from the proud and revealing it to little children (Luke 10:21)
- [[Thank Father at Lazarus]] - thanks the Father aloud at the tomb so the crowd would believe (John 11:41-42)
- [[Glorify Your Name]] - facing the cross, asks the Father to glorify His name (John 12:28)

## Surrender and intercession

- [[Not My Will]] - Gethsemane: "not what I will, but what You will" (Matthew 26:39; Mark 14:36; Luke 22:42)
- [[Pray for His Own]] - John 17: prayer for Himself, the twelve, and all future believers

## From the cross

- [[Father Forgive Them]] - "Father, forgive them" (Luke 23:34)
- [[Into Your Hands]] - "Father, into Your hands I commit My spirit" (Luke 23:46)

Matthew and Mark also record Psalm 22 on the cross - "My God, My God, why have You forsaken Me?" - a cry of dereliction, not a separate hub note here.
