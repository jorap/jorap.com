  Jesus lifted Leviticus 19:18 to equal rank with loving God. (Matthew 22:39, Mark 12:31). The parable of the Good Samaritan (Luke 10) reframes the question - not "who counts?" but "who acted as neighbor?" The standard is the care you'd want if you were hurt on the road. [[Mercy]] shows up in the body - bandage, ride, cost covered. [[Free Grace]] doesn't erase the command - it frees me to love without keeping score for salvation.
