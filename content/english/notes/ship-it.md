**Ship it** = publish the good-enough version instead of polishing forever.

## Examples

- **Sports:** We ran the new play ugly in scrimmage instead of drilling it another month - feedback beat perfection on the whiteboard.
- **Non-technical work:** The menu went live with one typo fixed after service - customers ate tonight instead of another week of proofreading.
- **Home with children:** The birthday slideshow shipped with one rough slide - the kid laughed at the candid; another month in iMovie would've missed the party.
- **Real world:** The blog post went live with one rough paragraph - feedback beat another month in drafts.
