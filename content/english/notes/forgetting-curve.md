**Forgetting curve** = memory fades fast without review — Ebbinghaus curve spaced repetition fights.

## Examples

- **Sports:** I forgot half the new footwork cues by Friday until I drilled five on the wall chart before every practice.
- **Non-technical work:** The new cashier forgot half the codes by Friday until the trainer taped five on the register during slow shifts.
- **Home with children:** Forgot half the vocab by Friday until I drilled five words on the fridge during rice.
- **Real world:** I forgot the neighbor's gate code by the second visit until I saved it where I'd see it on errand day - memory fades fast without review.
