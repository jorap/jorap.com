**Garden link issues** = broken wikilinks and note titles mentioned in prose without a `[[wikilink]]` - listed below and on each affected note. Faith parallel: [[Judge Not]] - fix the speck in the graph without playing judge over the author.

**Missing note** means the wikilink target does not match any published note. **Pipe wikilink** means deprecated double-bracket syntax with a pipe label (`[[target|label]]`) - use `[[Note Title]]` only. **Unlinked mention** means another note's title appears in prose without a link — not in markdown headings (`## …`) or the **Note Relationships** table. **Structure issues** flag missing `extends` / `contradicts` rows in the **Note Relationships** table on garden notes (utility/meta pages are exempt). Frontmatter and flashcards are not checked - only the note body.
