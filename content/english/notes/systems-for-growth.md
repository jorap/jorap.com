## Mindset

- [[Growth Mindset]] - skills train; a miss is data, not identity
- [[Failure as Feedback]] - treat the miss as instrument reading, not a verdict

## Habits and loops

- [[Habit Formation]] - repeat until the move runs without a daily debate
- [[Habit Stacking]] - piggyback a new habit on one you already do
- [[Process Over Outcomes]] - trust the loop when the scoreboard is quiet
- [[Compounding]] - boring repetition until the curve bends
- [[Discipline]] - keep the commitment when mood and applause aren't there
- [[Build a Reliable Default]] - under stress you fall back on what's most practiced

## Systems and procedures

- [[Systems Thinking]] - fix the loop, not only the symptom
- [[Standard Operating Procedures]] - written defaults the team can run without improvising

## Practice and skill

- [[Deliberate Practice]] - train the weak slice on purpose
- [[Minimum Effective Dose]] - smallest change that still moves the needle
- [[The Feynman Technique]] - teach it back to find the gap
- [[Spaced Repetition]] - review on rhythm before you forget

## Review and improvement

- [[Continuous Improvement]] - small fixes to process, repeated
- [[Weekly Review Checklists]] - Sunday pass that closes open loops
- [[Periodic Knowledge Review]] - quarterly sweep so notes don't rot
- [[Convert Pain Into Learning]] - failure only helps when it changes the next run
- [[Own the Error]] - name the miss and fix the process that allowed it

## Sustainable pace

- [[Sustainable Performance]] - a pace you can repeat next month
- [[Recovery]] - refill judgment before the next hard push
- [[Slow Productivity]] - fewer deep projects, finished work
- [[Energy Protects Judgment]] - fatigue breaks patience and decisions
- [[Develop, Don't Endanger]] - challenge without disproportionate risk

## Planning rhythms

- [[The 12 Week Year]] - quarterly intensity you can actually feel
- [[Adaptability]] - strength in one room can fail when conditions shift
