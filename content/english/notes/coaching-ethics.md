**Coaching ethics** = develop the person without hiding behind their consent when the drill is unsafe or pointless.

## Examples

- I wouldn't let my kid do a backflip off the couch because she asked twice - develop the person, don't hide behind consent when the move is reckless.
- The coach who said "they signed the waiver" after a near-drown wasn't coaching - he dodged [[Duty of Care]] when the drill was unsafe and pointless.
- The swim instructor kept pushing breath-hold games after a kid panicked - "parent consented" doesn't erase the job of picking a safer drill.
- The gym sold a dangerous cleanse because customers clicked agree - coaching ethics means you still owe reasonable protection to people under your influence.
