## Lord's Prayer and persistence

The disciples asked Him to teach them (Luke 11:1). He gave them words and a rhythm.

- [[Ask Seek Knock]] - Lord's Prayer order (Matthew 6:9-13; Luke 11:2-4); ask, seek, knock (Matthew 7:7-11; Luke 11:9-13)
- Luke 11:5-8 - friend at midnight; keep asking
- Luke 18:1-8 - persistent widow; pray and do not lose heart

Garden link: [[Periodic Knowledge Review]] - scheduled return, not one crisis burst.

## Secret audience

- [[Secret Devotion]] - do not pray for applause; pray in secret for the Father who sees (Matthew 6:5-8)

Garden link: [[Let Your Light Shine]] - public good works and hidden piety are different lanes.

## Posture before God

- [[Justification]] - Pharisee vs. tax collector; humility, not self-promotion (Luke 18:9-14)
- [[Childlike Faith]] - receive the kingdom like a child; prayer is not merit theater

## Who to pray for

- [[Love Your Enemies]] - bless and pray for persecutors (Matthew 5:44; Luke 6:28)
- Matthew 9:38; Luke 10:2 - pray that the Lord of the harvest would send laborers

## Asking in His name

- [[Abide in Me]] - remain in Him; ask what you wish (John 15:7, 16)
- John 14:13-14; 16:23-24 - ask the Father in Jesus' name

## Together and when you pray

- Matthew 18:19-20 - agreement in prayer; where two or three gather in His name
- Mark 11:24-25 - believe when you pray; forgive when you stand praying
- [[Forgiveness]] - forgive others in the rhythm of prayer (Matthew 6:14-15)

## Watch and pray

- Matthew 26:41 - watch and pray lest you enter temptation (Gethsemane)
- Matthew 21:13 - My house shall be called a house of prayer (Isaiah 56:7)
